2020-2023: Statistical Consulting Service (SCS)

Department of Statistics
Faculty of Applied Sciences
University of Sri Jayewardenepura

The Department of Statistics, Faculty of Applied Sciences, University of Sri Jayewardenepura provides a free statistical consulting service to university researchers and industrial organizations. We can assist clients in a diverse range of statistical issues. The statistical consulting service provides one-to-one statistical support.
